What Choose

Wind energy

Wind energy is the kinetic energy of air moving through the Earth's atmosphere. Wind energy is an indirect form of solar energy. Using wind energy is one of the oldest ways of obtaining energy from the natural environment and has been known since ancient times.

Wave energy

Wave energy is capturing the energy of ocean waves to do useful work - for example, generate electricity, desalinate water or pump water. The machine that harnesses wave energy is called a wave energy converter.

Solar energy

Solar energy, which radiates light and heat from the Sun, has been harnessed by humans since ancient times using an ever-evolving range of technologies.
